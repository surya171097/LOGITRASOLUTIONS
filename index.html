About Us

Logitra Solutions is a Service Company, incorporated as a Private Limited company in May 2011 to serve its customers their futuristic Logistics need, Consultancy and help Cost Savings. Also specializes in providing logical and transparent logistics solutions tailored for B2B businesses. We ensure timely and reliable road freight services across India.

Our Mission

To become 100% growth oriented services conglomerate of pride people always in action for solutions of service related problems of logistics and transport industry as a whole, and remain in leading role always. While doing so not to forget human dignity and believe in service with smile to maintain customers scarcity about its services. To meet the challenges and particularly customers demand in the various industrial clusters and evolve itself to meet those challenges in coming years.

Our Vision

Logitra Solutions is more a solution providing commercial conglomerate of skilled people of where everyone has placed to serve the customers best so that to maintain their scarcity about companies products and services forever. While doing so Logitra pledges to also maintain the enthusiasm of its employees, stakeholders, service partners and suppliers. At Logitra we believe in strengthening the relationships as it takes time to build them and once built it must be enjoyed by generations.

Quality Policy

Logitra Solutions working as bridge between businesses pledges always to provide best quality of services of world class industries into this field and thus adopt and act with new ideas and equipments to match the best in the industry.
